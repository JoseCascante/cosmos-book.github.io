In the book we have an infographic showing the total amount of time every astronaut has been in space (defined as above 100km). With Javascript, this page would show a summary of the compiled data set of astronauts or as json. You can browse our data for each astronaut on Github.

Only includes humans who have reached altitudes of at least 100 km. Flight durations are based on take-off/landing so short flights over-estimate the time in space. Based on data compiled from the NASA Information Summaries Astronaut Fact Book, the NASA History Office, spacefacts.de and other sources for Cosmos: The Infographic Book of Space / Stuart Lowe & Chris North
