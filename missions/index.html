Interplanetary missions

This graphic illustrates every mission to a body other than the Moon. Many of the early missions to Mars and Venus failed either on the launch pad or en-route. Some spacecraft have visited multiple objects; often using flybys to reach their final destination. A small number of missions have returned samples to the Earth. In this graphic, the order of planned arrival is shown by successively larger circles around the object in question.

  • Asteroid / comet
